Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

Introduction

Vin ("we", "our", or "us") is a wine cellar management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Vin:

  • Account Information: Your username and password (securely hashed) when you create an account.
  • Wine Collection Data: Details about wines you add to your cellar, including wine name, winery, vintage, region, varietal, purchase information, tasting notes, and ratings.
  • Bottle Inventory: Information about individual bottles in your collection, including location, quantity, purchase price, and consumption history.
  • Photos: Images of wine labels that you capture or upload for AI-powered label recognition.
  • Conversation Data: Messages you exchange with the AI Sommelier feature.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create and authenticate your account using JSON Web Tokens (JWT).
  • Wine Collection Management: To store and display your wine cellar inventory.
  • AI Label Scanning: To identify wines from photos of their labels using artificial intelligence.
  • AI Sommelier: To provide personalized wine recommendations and advice based on your collection.
  • Data Import/Export: To allow you to import wine data from CSV files and export your collection.

Photo Processing

When you use the label scanning feature, photos of wine labels are sent to our server for AI processing. These photos are processed in real time and are not stored permanently on our servers. The image data is transmitted to our AI service provider solely for the purpose of identifying the wine, and is discarded after the response is generated.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your wine collection data is stored in a database on the server associated with your app instance.
  • Passwords are securely hashed before storage and are never stored in plain text.
  • Authentication is managed via JWT (JSON Web Tokens), which are stored locally on your device.
  • We use HTTPS to encrypt data in transit between your device and our servers.

Third-Party Services

Vin uses the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): We use Anthropic's Claude AI to power our label scanning and AI Sommelier features. When you scan a wine label or chat with the AI Sommelier, relevant data (photo or message text, and your cellar context) is sent to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle this data. For more information, visit Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your account and wine collection data for as long as your account is active. Photos submitted for label scanning are processed in real time and are not retained. AI Sommelier conversation history is stored to provide continuity in your interactions and can be cleared at any time from the app.

Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To request data deletion, please contact us at reededwards92@gmail.com. We will process your request promptly and confirm once your data has been deleted.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to data portability (via our export feature)
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Vin is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
