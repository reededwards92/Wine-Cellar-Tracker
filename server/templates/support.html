Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vin?

Vin is a wine cellar management app that helps you track your wine collection, scan bottle labels with AI, get personalized recommendations from an AI sommelier, and keep a record of the wines you've enjoyed.

How does the AI Sommelier work?

The AI Sommelier is a conversational assistant that knows your cellar. You can ask it for pairing suggestions, tasting notes, recommendations, or have it help you manage your collection — all through natural conversation.

How do I scan a wine bottle?

Tap the camera button in the center of the tab bar. Point your camera at the wine label and take a photo. The AI will identify the wine and give you options to learn more, add it to your cellar, or view it if it's already in your collection.

Can I import my existing collection?

Yes. Vin supports importing wine data from CellarTracker CSV exports. Go to Settings and use the Import option to bring in your existing collection.

Is my data stored securely?

Your wine data is stored on the server associated with your app instance. Photos sent for label scanning are processed in real time and are not stored permanently.
